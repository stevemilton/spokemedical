About Spoke Medical

Pioneering precision medical devices that improve patient outcomes

Our Story

Spoke Medical was founded in 2018 by the original team behind Arc Medical Design Ltd., the innovators of Endocuff Vision, a globally adopted endoscopic tool that has prevented over 20,000 cancers and saved 10,000 lives.

Our Mission

At Spoke Medical, we're dedicated to transforming healthcare through innovative medical devices that address unmet clinical needs. We combine deep clinical expertise with cutting-edge engineering to create solutions that improve patient outcomes, enhance clinician experience, and deliver value to healthcare systems.

Our Vision

We envision a future where medical technology seamlessly integrates into clinical practice, making procedures safer, more efficient, and more comfortable for both patients and healthcare providers. Our goal is to set new standards in medical device innovation across multiple specialties.

Our Success Story: Endocuff Vision

Before developing Zephyr, our team created Endocuff Vision, a revolutionary endoscopic device that transformed colorectal cancer screening.

Global Impact

Endocuff Vision became the standard of care in colonoscopy procedures worldwide, with proven clinical benefits:

  • Increased adenoma detection rates by 10.8%
  • Prevented over 20,000 interval cancers
  • Saved an estimated 10,000 lives
  • Adopted by leading hospitals across 34 countries

The company was successfully acquired by Olympus Corporation in 2018, demonstrating our team's ability to develop, commercialize, and scale innovative medical technologies.
