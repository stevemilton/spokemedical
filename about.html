About Spoke Medical

Our story, mission, and the team behind our innovations

Our Story

At Spoke Medical, we create innovative, precision medical devices that improve patient outcomes and enhance the effectiveness of healthcare professionals.

Spoke Medical was founded in 2018 by the original team behind Arc Medical Design Ltd., the innovators of Endocuff Vision, a globally adopted endoscopic tool that has prevented over 20,000 cancers and saved 10,000 lives. The success of Endocuff, which was acquired by Olympus US in 2020, reflects our core strength: translating clinical insight into devices that elevate standards of care.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to identify persistent challenges in procedural medicine and to solve them through innovation that is practical, scalable, and grounded in real-world needs. Spoke Medical is committed to advancing care by listening to clinicians, protecting patients, and delivering exceptional value across the healthcare ecosystem.

Leadership Team

Our team combines decades of clinical excellence, regulatory expertise, and business leadership to redefine what's possible in healthcare.

Patrick Axon

Consultant Otologist

Patrick Axon is a distinguished otologist with over 30 years of experience in managing chronic ear disease, skull base pathology, and hearing loss. A former President of the British Skull Base Society, he has led national ear audits and specialist advisory groups. Patrick has played a key role in clinical evaluation and product refinement of Zephyr.

Neil Donnelly

Consultant ENT Surgeon

Neil Donnelly is a globally recognised authority on chronic ear disease and complex hearing loss. A pioneer of advanced endoscopic skull base surgery, Neil has lectured internationally and continues to contribute to the evolution of hearing preservation techniques. His frontline clinical perspective has been central to the development of Zephyr.

Executive Team

Combined Expertise

Our executive team comprises ENT Surgeons, Regulatory lawyers and marketing experts. Together, we combine decades of clinical excellence, legal mastery, business leadership, and technological ingenuity to redefine what's possible in healthcare.
