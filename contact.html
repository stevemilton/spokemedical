Frequently Asked Questions

How can I learn more about investment opportunities?

For detailed information about investment opportunities, please contact our investor relations team using the form above or email investors@spokemedical.com. We can provide additional information about our business model, growth strategy, and potential returns.

Are you looking for distribution partners?

Yes, we are actively seeking distribution partners in key markets. If you're interested in becoming a distribution partner for Zephyr, please contact our business development team with details about your company and market coverage.

When will Zephyr be available for purchase?

Zephyr is currently in the final stages of preparation for commercial launch in the UK market, with expansion to additional markets planned in the coming years. For specific availability information in your region, please contact our sales team.

Do you offer demonstrations of Zephyr?

Yes, we offer product demonstrations to qualified healthcare professionals and potential distribution partners. Please contact us to arrange a demonstration or to discuss your specific requirements.
