Zephyr: Redefining Ear Care

The First Innovation in Microsuction Technology in Over 50 Years

Product Overview

Zephyr is a next-generation microsuction device engineered to solve the clinical, acoustic, and ergonomic challenges of traditional ear wax removal. Developed by Spoke Medical in the UK, in close collaboration with ENT surgeons and audiologists, Zephyr combines precision suction with patient-centered safety.

Key Innovations

  • Operates at ≤75 dB - dramatically reducing noise-induced complications
  • Complete silence when idle - improving clinic environment
  • Precision airflow control - enhancing clinician control and patient comfort
  • Anti-block technology - minimizing procedure interruptions
  • Ergonomic design - reducing clinician fatigue
  • Fully sterilized single-use packs - eliminating cross-infection risk

Clinical Benefits

Zephyr addresses critical clinical risks identified by ENT UK while improving workflow and patient outcomes.

Mitigating Key Clinical Risks

In November 2024, ENT UK released updated clinical guidance on microsuction of the external ear canal, identifying critical risks with legacy equipment that Zephyr directly addresses:

Clinical Risk Traditional Devices Zephyr Solution
Excessive Noise Up to 140 dB — linked to patient distress, tinnitus, and potential hearing damage ≤75 dB operation with silent idle state
Rigid Metal Tips Uncomfortable and potentially injurious in narrow or sensitive canals Flexible, patient-friendly tips with safety design
Uncontrolled Suction Can trigger vertigo and caloric-induced inner ear trauma Precision fingertip airflow control
Frequent Blockages Lead to delays, repeated tool changes, and longer procedure times Anti-block technology minimizes interruptions
Clinician Fatigue Driven by acoustic load, poor ergonomics, and procedural inefficiency Ergonomic design with reduced noise and improved handling

Preventing Complications and Improving Clinical Workflow

Reduced Procedure Time

Anti-block technology and consistent suction power minimize interruptions, allowing for faster and more efficient procedures.

Fewer Complications

Precision control and reduced noise significantly decrease the risk of vertigo, tinnitus, and trauma during procedures.

Improved Patient Experience

Quieter operation and enhanced comfort lead to reduced anxiety and better overall patient satisfaction.

Enhanced Clinic Efficiency

Faster procedures and fewer complications allow for higher patient throughput and more efficient use of clinical resources.

Greater Confidence in Patient Safety

Zephyr is engineered to prevent common adverse events including vertigo, trauma, and tinnitus. It significantly improves the experience for both general and high-risk patients.

Lower Litigation Risk

Fewer complications lead to fewer complaints. Zephyr aligns with updated ENT UK guidance on consent, safety, and acoustic exposure.

Improved Patient Trust & Retention

A more comfortable experience leads to better compliance and return visits, creating a positive feedback loop: better outcomes → happier patients → more referrals.

Improving Everyday Clinical Excellence

Reduced Cognitive Load

Low-noise operation reduces clinician fatigue and improves concentration. Enables better communication with nurses due to overall lower in-clinic noise.

Faster, More Reliable Procedures

Anti-block design minimises tip clearing and interruptions. Keeps suction consistent—even with fine debris—allowing for higher daily throughput.

Ergonomic and Intuitive Handling

Lightweight and balanced with fingertip suction control. Reduces hand strain, especially during complex or extended procedures.

Minimal Training Required

Plug-and-play usability with no learning curve. Seamlessly integrates into current workflows and clinic setup.
