Pricing Strategy

Our pricing strategy is designed to maximize market penetration while ensuring strong margins:

  • Premium Positioning: Zephyr is priced as a premium product reflecting its superior technology and clinical benefits
  • Value-Based Pricing: Pricing reflects the significant value created through improved clinical outcomes and efficiency
  • Tiered Approach: Multiple product configurations at different price points to address various market segments
  • Volume Discounts: Strategic pricing for high-volume customers and institutional buyers

This approach enables strong gross margins of 65-70% while remaining competitive in the market.
